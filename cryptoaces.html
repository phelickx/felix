CryptoAces

A decentralized poker game

A place made for the billionaires.








Problem

To Solve the problems in Other gaming application where a player don’t get to interect with other players without loosing focus on the game and also to provide a seecure decentralized gaming experience



Solution

As money in Involved, the application needs to be secured to reduce the percentage of risks a user might incure, So the app was built to be a decentralized system where a the app determines the winner and even the Owner of the App doesnt have “That Ownership” access to the App. Secondly the User gets to chat and Communicate with other players thereby making the app Interesting and less boring. Players also can open upto 5 games at the same time and can participate in all without any Problem because the app has a game notifier that always blinks to call the user attention on other games.
